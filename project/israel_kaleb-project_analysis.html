Project Analysis

  • Client: Get Gaming Orlando
  • Project: Website Redesign
  • Designer: Kaleb Israel

Site Intent

The website redesign goal for Get Gaming Orlando is to create a clear call to action and create a dominant content focus.

By creating a clear call to action, users will be more likely to become a customer. By creating a dominant content focus, users will be able to recognize the website purpose and are more likely to stay interested if they understand the services provided.

Competitive Analysis

Through research I have found that Get Gaming Orlando is pulling in audience interested in microsoft services and software. Get Gaming Orlando is not a microsoft store and needs to be recognized for the game repair services that they offer.

Website Keywords

get gaming orlando, orlando, game console repair, game repair shops, xbox 360, playstation 3, xbox repair, ps3 repair, console repair, broken wii

Popular Demographics

  • Age 18-24
  • Male
  • Income $0 - 30k
  • No children

Website Statistics

  • Average time spent on popular system repair websites is just over 2 minutes
  • Average pageviews equals 1.6
  • Average bounce rate is around 71%

Targeted Audience

Our target audience is primarily male of the age 18-24 with an income of less than $30k. These men have some college experience and do not have children to take care of.

User Profile

Joshua works a part time job and spends a lot of time playing video games in his off time. He has some college education and is familiar with electronic devices. He does not want to void his warranty or spend a lot of money to fix his broken system.
